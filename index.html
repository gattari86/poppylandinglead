Common Questions

How much does web design cost? +

Website projects typically start at $2,500 and go up depending on complexity, features, and functionality. Every project is custom quoted based on your specific needs and goals. We'll provide a transparent estimate during your consultation.

Can you help with my brand identity? +

Absolutely! We offer logo design starting at $350, brand guidelines, color palette development, and complete brand positioning. Whether you need a fresh rebrand or to define your identity from scratch, we can help.

Do you manage social media accounts? +

Yes! Our social media management includes monthly content planning, daily posting, community engagement, and analytics reporting. Monthly retainers start at $1,000. We handle everything so you can focus on running your business.

What's your process for paid ads? +

We handle Facebook and Instagram ads from strategy through execution. This includes audience research, ad creative design, ongoing optimization, and detailed reporting. We focus on ROI and measurable results.

How often will I see updates? +

Weekly check-ins are standard on all projects. You'll get regular updates on progress, metrics, and next steps. Communication and transparency are core to how we work.

Do you work with businesses outside Houston? +

While we specialize in serving Houston-area businesses, we work with clients nationwide. Many of our services are 100% remote-friendly. Let's discuss your specific needs.
