Common Questions

What's the minimum ad spend required?

The minimum is $600/month (paid directly to Google, not to us). However, for most competitive industries in Houston, we recommend budgeting $1,500-$3,500/month to get faster data and more leads. Our management fees start at $499/month.

Why does your management fee scale with ad spend?

Simple: bigger budgets require more work. A client spending $600/month generates ~20 clicks/day to analyze. A client spending $6,000/month generates ~200 clicks/day. That's 10x the data to manage and optimize. Our tiers range from $499/month to $699/month, with custom pricing for Enterprise clients.

Do I own the landing page and ads?

Yes. You own everything. The landing page copy, design files, and Google Ads account are yours. If you decide to leave, you keep everything and can take it to another agency.

How fast can we go live?

Once we complete your strategy call and get your approval, we can typically have your landing page built and ads live within 48-72 hours. Most agencies take 2-3 weeks.

I serve clients outside Houston. Can you still help?

Absolutely. While we're based in Katy and specialize in the Houston market, Google Ads works anywhere. We can target any city, zip code, or state in the US.

What if I'm already running Google Ads?

Great! We offer a $800 Conversion Tracking Audit where we analyze your existing setup, identify what's broken, and show you exactly what needs to be fixed. Book a strategy call and we'll take a quick look for free.

Are there any long-term contracts?

No. The $1,299 launch package is a one-time payment. After that, monthly management is month-to-month. You can cancel anytime with 30 days' notice.

What kind of results should I expect?

Results depend on your industry, competition, and spending. We can't guarantee specific leads because every market is different. What we CAN guarantee is daily optimization, transparent reporting every week, and cutting wasted spend immediately. Most clients see qualified leads within 7-14 days.
